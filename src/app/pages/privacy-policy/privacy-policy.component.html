info Overview

This Privacy Policy describes how uma.moe ("we," "our," or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our Umamusume resource hub (the "Service"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and complying with applicable data protection laws, including the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

data_usage Data We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Account Data: When you sign in via Discord or other OAuth providers, we receive your username and provider ID. We do not receive or store your password.
  • Linked Game Accounts: Trainer IDs you voluntarily link to your account for verification and rankings.
  • API Keys: Keys you generate to access our API programmatically.
  • User Preferences: Filters, settings, and customization choices stored in your browser.

Information Collected Automatically

  • Technical Data: IP address (for security and rate limiting), browser type, device information.
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, basic navigation patterns - used solely for maintaining the Service.
  • Security Logs: Access logs for abuse prevention, retained for a limited period.

What We Don't Collect

  • We do not use tracking pixels or fingerprinting.
  • We do not collect detailed behavioral profiles.
  • We do not collect precise location data.

person Accounts & Authentication

OAuth Sign-In

We use third-party OAuth providers (such as Discord) for authentication. When you sign in, we receive a limited profile from the provider (username, provider user ID). We never receive or store your password from any provider.

Linked Game Accounts

You may optionally link your in-game Trainer ID to your account. This is used for verification and to display your trainer name in rankings. Linked Trainer IDs and associated public game data (trainer name, representative character) are visible to other users.

API Keys

You can generate API keys to authenticate with our API. Keys are stored as hashed values; the full key is shown only once at creation. We log request counts per key for rate limiting.

cookie Cookies & Local Storage

We use cookies and browser local storage to operate the Service. When you first visit, a consent banner lets you choose which optional cookie categories to enable. You can change your preferences at any time via the cookie icon in the bottom-left corner or from the Cookie Settings link in the footer.

Essential (Always On)

Required for the site to function. These cannot be disabled.

  • Authentication token (JWT) for keeping you signed in
  • Cookie consent preferences
  • UI preferences (theme, filters, etc.)

Analytics (Optional - Consent Required)

Help us understand how visitors use the site so we can improve it.

  • Anonymous page view counts and navigation patterns
  • Performance monitoring and error tracking

Advertising (Optional - Consent Required)

Used to show relevant ads and measure their effectiveness, when advertising is enabled.

  • Ad personalization cookies from ad networks
  • Conversion tracking

Managing Cookies

Besides our consent banner, you can also control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling essential cookies may prevent the site from functioning correctly. You can open cookie settings to change your preferences now.

ads_click Advertising

We do not currently display advertisements. If we introduce ads in the future, the following will apply:

  • Advertising cookies will only be activated with your explicit consent.
  • You will be able to opt out at any time through our cookie settings.
  • No personally identifiable information will be shared with ad networks without your consent.

Opting Out

  • Google Ad Settings
  • Digital Advertising Alliance
  • Use our cookie settings to disable advertising cookies

gavel Your Rights (GDPR)

If you are in the European Union or EEA, you have the following rights:

Access Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
Rectification Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
Erasure Request deletion of your personal data ("right to be forgotten").
Restrict Processing Request that we limit how we use your data.
Data Portability Receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format.
Object Object to processing based on legitimate interests or direct marketing.
Withdraw Consent Withdraw consent at any time where processing is based on consent (e.g. cookies).
Complain Lodge a complaint with your local data protection authority.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the address in the Contact section. We will respond within 30 days.

schedule Data Retention

  • Account Data: Retained while your account exists. Deleted upon request.
  • Linked Game Accounts: Retained until you unlink them or delete your account.
  • API Keys: Retained until you revoke them or delete your account.
  • User Preferences: Stored in your browser until you clear site data.
  • Security Logs: Retained for up to 12 months, then deleted.

When data is no longer needed, we securely delete or anonymize it.

security Security

  • Encryption: All data in transit is protected by TLS.
  • Authentication: Passwords are never stored; authentication is delegated to OAuth providers.
  • API Keys: Stored as hashed values; the plaintext is never retained.
  • Access Controls: Personal data access is limited on a need-to-know basis.
  • Breach Notification: In the event of a breach, we will notify affected users and relevant authorities within 72 hours.

child_care Children's Privacy

Our Service is not intended for children under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will remove it.

update Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Significant changes will be announced on the site and the "Last Updated" date will be revised. For material changes affecting your GDPR rights, we will seek your explicit consent where required.
